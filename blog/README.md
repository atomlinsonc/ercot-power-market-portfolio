# Blog and Learning Log

This folder contains the public writing that supports the ERCOT Power Market Analytics Portfolio.

The writing has two purposes:

1. Technical development logs explain what was built, what broke, what was learned, and how the code supports a repeatable analyst workflow.
2. Market observation notes explain what the dashboard shows and what questions a trading desk or market analyst might ask next.

Early posts use mock ERCOT-style data. They should not imply that live ERCOT data is connected until that integration is actually complete.

## Recommended Cadence

| Content type | Frequency | Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Technical devlog | Weekly | 500-900 words |
| Market observation | 2-3 times per week | 200-400 words |
| Monthly reflection | Once/month | 700-1,200 words |
