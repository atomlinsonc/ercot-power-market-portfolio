# Week 4: Day-Ahead vs Real-Time Spreads

Date: 2026-06
Category: ERCOT Learning Notes

## What I worked on

I added charts and summary calculations for day-ahead versus real-time price spreads. The dashboard uses DA minus RT as the spread measure so positive and negative values can be interpreted consistently.

## Why it matters

DA/RT spreads can highlight where real-time conditions differed from expectations. For a trading or hedging workflow, those differences can point to forecast misses, congestion, scarcity conditions, or other market drivers.

## What I learned

The phrase "market miss" needs careful use. A spread does not explain itself. It points to a difference that deserves follow-up questions about load, generation, weather, outages, congestion, and market rules.

## Next improvement

Add ranked intervals for largest positive and negative spreads and compare spread behavior across hubs and load zones.
