# Week 7: Dashboard Polish and Lessons

Date: 2026-07
Category: Dashboard Improvement

## What I worked on

I focused on dashboard presentation: clearer chart titles, cleaner labels, simpler layout, and more useful summary text.

## What makes a chart useful

A chart should answer a question quickly. The best dashboard sections are not the flashiest ones; they are the ones that help someone identify what changed and where to look next.

## Lessons learned

For this project, useful charts include real-time price over time, day-ahead versus real-time comparison, DA/RT spread, and load forecast versus actual load. Extra visuals should earn their place by supporting a desk question.
