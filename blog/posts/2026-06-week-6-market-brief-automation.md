# Week 6: Market Brief Automation

Date: 2026-06
Category: Technical Devlog

## What I worked on

I started turning the dashboard output into a daily market brief. The brief summarizes price levels, spike intervals, DA/RT spreads, load forecast error, and items to watch.

## Why it matters for a trading desk

A junior analyst often creates value by making information easier to act on. A repeatable market brief can help a desk start the day with the same basic facts: what moved, what was unusual, and what needs more investigation.

## What I learned technically

The same functions that power a dashboard can also power a report. Keeping summary calculations in reusable modules makes it easier to build both visual and written outputs.

## What I would improve next

The report should eventually be generated from live public data and saved automatically with a timestamped filename.
