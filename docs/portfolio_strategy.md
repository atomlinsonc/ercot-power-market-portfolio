# Portfolio Strategy

## Positioning

This portfolio is designed for ERCOT power-market analyst, trading support, market operations, asset optimization, scheduling, and risk analyst roles. The goal is to show practical desk-support capability rather than claim trading experience.

## Public Story

I am a high school economics teacher and data science master's student building applied ERCOT analytics projects with public market data. The portfolio shows how I structure data, build repeatable analysis, explain market behavior, and communicate clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.

## Portfolio Flow

1. Homepage
2. Featured Project: ERCOT Real-Time Market Dashboard
3. Live Dashboard
4. Build Log + Market Notes
5. GitHub Repo
6. Resume / Contact

## Writing Cadence

| Content type | Frequency | Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Technical devlog | Weekly | 500-900 words |
| Market observation | 2-3 times per week | 200-400 words |
| Monthly reflection | Once/month | 700-1,200 words |

## Interview Narrative

"I built a live ERCOT dashboard, but I also documented the process. The blog shows both the technical work and the market questions the dashboard helped me ask. My goal was to build the kind of daily workflow a junior trading analyst or desk-support analyst could maintain."
