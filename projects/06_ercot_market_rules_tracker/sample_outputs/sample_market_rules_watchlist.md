# ERCOT Market Rules / NPRR Watchlist

Source notice: This sample watchlist uses public ERCOT source links and manually entered starter records. Status and next-step fields should be verified against ERCOT before publication or interview use.

## Executive Summary

The sample tracker organizes selected ERCOT NPRR items into a desk-support format. Each item includes a topic, impact area, source link, priority, and analyst note so rule research can connect back to dashboard interpretation and market commentary.

## Topic Notes

### Energy Storage Resource

Storage-related rules matter for the Battery Arbitrage Simulator because rule changes can affect how storage is modeled, mitigated, or discussed in market commentary.

### Ancillary Services

Ancillary service changes matter because reserve products and reliability services can affect scarcity interpretation, opportunity cost, and dashboard watch items.

### RUC / Reliability Unit Commitment

RUC-related rules provide operational context that may help explain price behavior or reliability actions during tight intervals.
